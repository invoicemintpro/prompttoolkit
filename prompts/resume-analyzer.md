# 📄 Resume Analyzer Prompt

Analyze resumes against a job description to identify skill gaps and provide improvement suggestions.

## Prompt

```text
Act as a Technical Recruiter. Compare the following Resume against the Job Description (JD).

Resume:
---
[INSERT RESUME TEXT]
---

Job Description:
---
[INSERT JD TEXT]
---

Analysis Task:
1. Match Score (0-100%).
2. Top 3 missing keywords/skills.
3. Suggestions to tailor the resume for this specific role.
4. Three potential interview questions based on the resume's experience.
```

## How to use
1. Paste the full text of the resume and the job posting.
2. This is useful for both job seekers (optimization) and hiring managers (initial screening).
