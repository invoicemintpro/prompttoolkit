# 🚀 Startup Pitch Generator Prompt

Craft compelling elevator pitches and slide deck outlines for your next big idea.

## Prompt

```text
You are a Venture Capitalist and Master Pitch Coach. I need a [30-second/3-minute] pitch for my startup: [STARTUP NAME].

Startup Concept: [DESCRIPTION]
Target Market: [WHO]
Revenue Model: [HOW YOU MAKE MONEY]

Deliverables:
- Hook: A powerful opening statement.
- The Problem & Solution.
- Market Size & Opportunity.
- Business Model & Traction.
- The "Ask" (Call to Action).
```

## How to use
1. Be bold about the problem you're solving.
2. Keep it concise; the goal is to get a second meeting, not explain every detail.
