# 🗺️ Product Roadmap Prompt

Generate clear, strategic product roadmaps for your SaaS or internal tool.

## Prompt

```text
Act as a Senior Product Manager. I need a [3-month/6-month] roadmap for [PROJECT NAME/DESCRIPTION].

Current State: [What's built so far]
Main Goals: [e.g., Launch MVP, scale to 1k users, integrate AI features]

Deliverables:
- Monthly themes or focus areas.
- High-level feature list per month.
- Key milestones and KPIs for each phase.
- Potential risks and dependency analysis.
```
