# 💡 SaaS Idea Generator Prompt

Generate unique, viable, and technical SaaS ideas based on current market trends and specific niches.

## Prompt

```text
You are a Serial Entrepreneur and Product Strategist. Generate 5 unique SaaS product ideas for the [NICHE, e.g., Real Estate, Remote Work, Healthcare] industry.

For each idea, provide:
1. Problem: The specific pain point being solved.
2. Solution: How the AI-powered SaaS solves it.
3. Target Audience: Who would pay for this?
4. Technical Feasibility: Key technologies needed (e.g., Vector DBs, Whisper API).
5. Monetization Strategy: Subscription, Per-seat, or Usage-based.
```

## How to use
1. Pick a niche you are passionate about or have expertise in.
2. Use this as a brainstorming starting point for your next project.
