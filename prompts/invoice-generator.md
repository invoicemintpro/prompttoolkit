# 💰 Invoice Generator Prompt

Transform raw billing data into professional, structured JSON or HTML for invoicing systems.

## Prompt

```text
You are a Financial Data Analyst. I have raw billing data that needs to be formatted into a professional invoice.

Raw Data:
---
[INSERT BILLING DATA, e.g., hours worked, rate per hour, client info]
---

Task:
1. Calculate the subtotal, taxes ([TAX RATE]%), and grand total.
2. Format the output as [JSON/HTML/Markdown].
3. Ensure the invoice includes a unique invoice number, date, and due date (Net 30).
```

## How to use
1. Paste your raw notes, CSV data, or unstructured text into the "Raw Data" section.
2. Specify the tax rate and desired output format.
