# 🚀 Workflow: From Idea to Production

This guide demonstrates how to chain the prompts in this toolkit to build a feature from scratch.

## Step 1: Idea & Architecture
1. Use [SaaS Idea Generator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/saas-idea-generator.md) to refine your concept.
2. Use [System Design](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/system-design.md) to define your stack.
3. Use [Database Schema](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/database-schema.md) to design your tables.

## Step 2: Backend Development
1. Use [API Generator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/api-generator.md) to scaffold your endpoints.
2. Use [SQL Query Generator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/sql-query-generator.md) for complex data fetching.
3. Use [Auth & Security Validator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/auth-validator.md) to secure your routes.

## Step 3: Quality & Testing
1. Use [Unit Test Generator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/unit-test-generator.md) for your logic.
2. Use [Cypress Test Generator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/cypress-test-generator.md) for E2E flows.
3. Use [Security Vulnerability Scanner](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/security-scanner.md) to audit the code.

## Step 4: Deployment & Documentation
1. Use [Dockerfile Generator](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/dockerfile-generator.md) to containerize.
2. Use [CI/CD Pipeline Builder](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/cicd-pipeline.md) to automate shipping.
3. Use [Documentation Writer](file:///e:/Githubrepo/prompttoolkit/prompts/documentation-writer.md) to finish the README.
